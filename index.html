Bacteria to Beef?
Research May Tell Us

Research is under way to discover and document potentially unknown bacteria that could synthesize proteins for the production of synthetic beef. Previously, meat has been synthesized from algae, fungi, and plant root microorganisms. The company, Improbable Beef, is looking for bacteria that may synthesize proteins that taste more like beef than previous attempts. After researching bacteria, they think that the one they need may be found on the human body, specifically in the belly button area. This dashboard will let you explore the various species found on the donors.
